Spanish SEO FAQ

Do you acquire backlinks from .es domains?

Yes. The Google.es algorithm heavily favors contextual links from Spanish IP addresses and .es domains. We conduct manual outreach to high-authority Spanish blogs, news sites, and industry portals to build a powerful, natural link profile.

Do you optimize content for Latin America or just Spain?

Spanish is spoken globally, but search intent varies drastically between Spain and LatAm. We use strict hreflang tags (e.g., hreflang="es-ES") and localized Spanish terminology to ensure Google knows your content is specifically targeting the European Spanish market.
