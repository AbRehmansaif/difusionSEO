Australian SEO FAQ

Why do I need .com.au backlinks?

Google's algorithm looks for geographic relevance. If you want to rank #1 in Australia, you need trust signals originating from Australia. We conduct targeted outreach to secure high Domain Rating (DR) backlinks from legitimate Australian publishers and industry sites.

How do you handle national vs local campaigns?

We separate the architecture. National campaigns utilize semantic clusters and programmatic content hubs targeting broad commercial intent. Local campaigns (e.g., targeting Sydney specifically) rely on localized landing pages, GBP signals, and geographic schema.
