Swiss SEO FAQ

How do you manage the 4 national languages of Switzerland?

We utilize advanced hreflang architectures (e.g., hreflang="de-CH", "fr-CH", "it-CH") and separate subdirectories. This ensures that a user searching in German from Zurich sees the German content, while a user in Geneva sees the French version, perfectly satisfying Google.ch intent without duplicate content penalties.

Do you acquire .ch backlinks?

Absolutely. The Google.ch algorithm relies heavily on regional trust signals. We conduct manual outreach to secure high Domain Rating (DR) backlinks from premium Swiss domains, local news portals, and authoritative industry networks.
